# Activities

Every rest, each player picks an activity from the grid. The module handles the mechanics - skill checks, resource tracking, GM approvals - so the GM doesn't have to manage each request individually.

Every activity has an "if combat occurs" badge showing how it affects initiative and surprise if a fight breaks out during the rest.

## Camp duties

Shared activities that protect the party.

![Keep Watch - +3 initiative, cannot be surprised](activity_keep_watch.png)

**Keep Watch** - Stand guard. +3 initiative, cannot be surprised. Party gains +1 initiative (alarm).

![Scout Perimeter - advantage on surprise saves](activity_scout_perimeter.png)

**Scout Perimeter** - Stealth check. Map escape routes, spot threats. Advantage on initiative and surprise saves. On success, party may choose starting positions.

![Set Up Defenses - encounter threshold reduced](activity_set_defenses.png)

**Set Up Defenses** - Group activity. Stealth check to set tripwires, cover tracks. Encounter threshold reduced by 2. On success, party gains +1 initiative (tripwire warning).

![Tend Wounds - Medicine check to heal a companion](activity_tend_wounds.png)

**Tend Wounds** - Medicine check. Target companion recovers an additional hit die worth of HP on success, small amount on failure. Normal combat readiness.

## Personal

Individual benefit activities.

![Training - ability check for XP](activity_training.png)

**Training** - Drill or practice. Ability check (highest score) against DC 13 for XP. Diminishing returns on consecutive rests. +2 initiative if combat occurs (warmed up).

![Rest Fully - comfort tier bonus](activity_rest_fully.png)

**Rest Fully** - Passive. Effective comfort level increased by one tier. Disadvantage on initiative and surprise saves (deep sleep).

![Forage - Survival check for materials](activity_forage.png)

**Forage** - Survival check for useful materials. -5 initiative if combat occurs (away from camp, scrambling back).

## Arcane

Spell-gated activities. These only appear in the grid if the character has the required spell.

![Identify Items - ritual scan for magical auras](activity_identify_scan.png)

**Identify Items (Scan)** - Casters with Detect Magic or Identify get a Scan button. Ritual cast during the rest, free action. Scans the party's gear for magical auras and lets the player identify items without consuming their activity slot.

![Attune Item - pick from un-attuned inventory](activity_attune_item.png)

**Attune Item** - Shows un-attuned items from inventory with slot tracking (X/3). Pick an item and attune during the rest. Disadvantage on initiative (meditation focus).

![Copy Spell - full PHB workflow](activity_copy_spell.png)

**Copy Spell** - Full PHB spell-copying workflow. Pick a spell level, system calculates gold cost (50gp per level), rolls Arcana against DC. GM gets a socket-based approval request for the source scroll. Gold deducted on success, scroll consumed regardless. Disadvantage on initiative and surprise saves (deep arcane concentration).

## Campfire

![Campfire panel with whittle mechanic](activity_campfire_whittle.png)

Interactive fire panel alongside the activity grid. Three stages: embers, campfire, bonfire. Players drag kindling from inventory and can whittle for extra fuel. Fire state affects encounter modifiers.

Reflection prompts (Reflect on the Day, Study Lore, Share a Story) provide RP moments with zero mechanical weight.
